Internal Medicine Physician - Primary Care

Company: HealthFirst Medical Group
Location: Boston, MA (On-site)
Employment Type: Full-time
Salary: $220,000 - $280,000 annually + benefits

About Us:
HealthFirst Medical Group is a leading healthcare provider serving the greater Boston area for over 30 years. We operate 15 primary care clinics and are committed to delivering exceptional patient-centered care. Our team of 200+ physicians serves over 500,000 patients annually.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Board-Certified Internal Medicine Physician to join our expanding primary care team. The ideal candidate will provide comprehensive medical care to adult patients, manage chronic conditions, and promote preventive health practices.

Responsibilities:
- Provide comprehensive primary care services to adult patients (ages 18+)
- Diagnose and treat acute and chronic medical conditions
- Develop and implement individualized treatment plans
- Manage a patient panel of approximately 1,800-2,000 patients
- Perform routine health screenings and preventive care assessments
- Order and interpret diagnostic tests and laboratory results
- Prescribe medications and therapies as appropriate
- Coordinate referrals to specialists when needed
- Maintain accurate and timely electronic medical records
- Participate in quality improvement initiatives
- Collaborate with interdisciplinary healthcare team members
- Mentor medical students and residents (optional)

Requirements:
- MD or DO degree from accredited medical school
- Completion of Internal Medicine residency program
- Board Certification in Internal Medicine (ABIM)
- Active Massachusetts Medical License (or ability to obtain)
- DEA registration
- Minimum 3 years of clinical experience preferred
- Proficiency with Electronic Health Records (Epic preferred)
- BLS and ACLS certification required
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Strong clinical judgment and decision-making abilities

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with population health management
- Bilingual (Spanish or Mandarin) highly valued
- Experience with telemedicine platforms
- Quality improvement or Lean Six Sigma training
- Academic teaching experience

Benefits:
- Competitive base salary with productivity bonus
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with 6% employer match
- CME allowance ($5,000 annually)
- Paid time off (4 weeks vacation + holidays)
- Malpractice insurance coverage
- Relocation assistance available
- Student loan repayment program
- Partnership track available after 3 years

Work Schedule:
- Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- One evening clinic per week (until 7:00 PM)
- Shared weekend call rotation (1:8)
- 32 patient contact hours per week
