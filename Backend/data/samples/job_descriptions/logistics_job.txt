Distribution Center Operations Manager

Company: GlobalFreight Logistics
Location: Chicago, IL (On-site)
Employment Type: Full-time
Salary: $95,000 - $125,000 + performance bonus

About GlobalFreight:
GlobalFreight Logistics is a leading third-party logistics (3PL) provider serving Fortune 500 companies across North America. With 25 distribution centers nationwide and a fleet of 2,000+ vehicles, we handle over 50 million shipments annually. We're committed to innovation, sustainability, and operational excellence.

Position Overview:
We are seeking an experienced Distribution Center Operations Manager to lead our 400,000 sq ft Chicago facility. You will oversee all aspects of warehouse operations including receiving, inventory management, order fulfillment, and shipping. This role requires a hands-on leader who can drive efficiency while maintaining safety and quality standards.

Key Responsibilities:
- Direct daily operations of distribution center with 150+ team members across multiple shifts
- Develop and execute operational strategies to meet productivity and accuracy targets
- Manage annual operating budget of $8M, identifying cost reduction opportunities
- Ensure compliance with safety regulations, maintaining zero lost-time injuries
- Lead continuous improvement initiatives using Lean and Six Sigma methodologies
- Oversee inventory accuracy, targeting 99.5%+ cycle count accuracy
- Monitor and optimize key performance indicators (KPIs) including:
  * Order accuracy (target: 99.8%)
  * On-time shipping (target: 98%)
  * Labor productivity (units per hour)
  * Dock-to-stock time
- Collaborate with transportation team on inbound/outbound scheduling
- Manage vendor relationships for equipment, supplies, and temporary staffing
- Implement and optimize warehouse management system (WMS)
- Recruit, train, and develop supervisory and hourly staff
- Conduct performance reviews and manage employee relations
- Coordinate with customers on service level agreements and special requirements
- Lead peak season planning and execution (Q4 holiday volume)
